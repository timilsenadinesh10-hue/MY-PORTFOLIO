About

A student developer who ships, not just studies.

I'm a BSc CSIT student with a habit of finishing what I start — from class assignments to side projects that outgrew the syllabus. My focus is full-stack web development with Python/Django, mobile apps with Flutter, and increasingly React on the front end.

What pulls me toward tech is the feedback loop: build something, watch real people use it, fix what's broken, repeat. Outside of code I care about clean UI, readable systems, and writing docs I'd actually want to read.
